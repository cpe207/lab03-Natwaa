This project has won national awards in the Process category and the Social Vote award for the 2565 Energy and Environmental Innovation Competition. The work utilizes a Micro Bit board to detect hotspots, clearly pinpointing forest fire origins for authorities. Additionally, it alerts about air quality through the Sensor For All app, contributing to reducing carbon dioxide emissions from burning processes.
